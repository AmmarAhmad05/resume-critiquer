# Resume Critiquer

An AI-powered resume analysis tool that provides intelligent feedback and actionable insights to help job seekers optimize their resumes for success.

---

## Overview

Resume Critiquer is a modern web application that leverages artificial intelligence to analyze and critique resumes. The platform helps job seekers understand their resume's strengths and weaknesses, identify areas for improvement, and receive data-driven recommendations to make their resume stand out to recruiters and pass ATS (Applicant Tracking System) screening.

---

## Features

- **AI-Powered Resume Analysis** - Advanced evaluation using natural language processing to identify strengths and weaknesses  
- **Real-time Feedback** - Get instant, actionable suggestions as you build or upload your resume  
- **ATS Compatibility Assessment** - Determine how well your resume will perform with automated applicant tracking systems  
- **Skill Gap Analysis** - Discover missing skills and receive recommendations for professional development  
- **Content Optimization Suggestions** - Get recommendations for stronger language, better structure, and impactful statements  
- **Job Description Matching** - Compare your resume against specific job postings to identify relevant keywords and skills  
- **Professional Insights** - Receive guidance on formatting, structure, and industry best practices  
- **Detailed Scoring Metrics** - Visual feedback with comprehensive scoring across different resume sections
